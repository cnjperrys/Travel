My Favorite Cafes in South Korea

Anthrocite Cafe

This place, located in Hannam-dong, Seoul, was certain from the beginning that we would make it our space. All the large windows of the old building can be opened to make it a passage. The music you hear and the taste and aroma of coffee can create another culture.

Address:

240 Itaewon-ro, Yongsan-gu, Seoul 1st – 3rd floors

Events and Happenings

Public Cupping Hannam Every Wednesday 7pm – 8pm Hannam branch 1st floor cupping bar Anyone can participate freely after applying on the website.
